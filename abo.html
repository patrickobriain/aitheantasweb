About Us

Aitheantas means Identity as Gaelige. Identity is something that is denied every adoptee regardless of where they were born. Aitheantas is on a mission to change that. Aitheantas is campaigning to give Irish adoptees access to their own information on Health, Heritage and History and acknowledgment of their Identity Rights.
